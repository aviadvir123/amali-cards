# Product Definition

## What It Is
A one-sided app on the customer's phone replacing Amali's paper punch cards. No barista app, no backend, no accounts.

## How It Works
- Customer opens the app and sees their punch card
- To earn a punch, the customer enters a code the barista tells them verbally
- Code is constant for MVP, later changeable remotely
- At 10 punches — celebration, then reset to zero
- No history of past cards. Literally a paper card replacement.
- Onboarding: customer scans a QR code on the countertop to open the app

## Constraints
- **Zero cost** — development, hosting, and production must be fully free
- **No app store** — must work without requiring an install from App Store / Play Store
